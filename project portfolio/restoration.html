About the project:

Game Engine: Unity

Category: Game Jam

Published: Game Jolt


Description of the game:

A turn-based game with elements of randomness, buying skills and passing stages.Restoration is a simple platformer game where your main goal is to collect sunflower seeds (which are considered the national flower of Ukraine) and sow them in the ground so that the sunflowers will grow. There are 25 sunflower seeds that symbolize 24 regions and 1 autonomous republic (Crimea), sowing and growing represents restoration and revitalization of a country touched by war. You can do it alone or you can ask for help another player and play this game in cooperation mode! Clothes on characters are inspired by Ukrainian folk costumes and music was written by Ukrainian citizen who used folk instruments in order to create folklore experience.
